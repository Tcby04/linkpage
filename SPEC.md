# LinkPage - Micro-SaaS Specification

## What It Is
A clean, simple "link in bio" landing page builder. Like Linktree but cheaper ($3/month vs $9/month) with a more minimal, professional look.

## Target Users
- Freelancers
- Small business owners
- Content creators
- Anyone who needs a simple landing page for their links

## Features

### Free Tier
- Profile: name, bio, avatar
- Up to 5 links
- 6 theme colors
- Local storage (no account needed to try)

### Pro Tier ($3/month)
- Unlimited links
- Custom background image
- Analytics (clicks, views)
- Custom domain
- Remove LinkPage branding

## Tech Stack
- Static HTML/CSS/JS
- LocalStorage for free tier
- Simple backend for Pro (optional, could use Firebase or simple JSON storage)

## Monetization
- $3/month for Pro features
- Stripe or Venmo for payments
- Could offer annual discount
